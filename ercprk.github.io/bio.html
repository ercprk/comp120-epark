About Eric Park

Early Life & Education

I was born in Busan, South Korea. I spent my childhood there except when I was 11 and lived in Melbourne, Australia for a few months to learn English. I came to the U.S. when I was 15. I finished my high school education and graduated from the McCallie School in Tennessee in 2016. I then went on to attend Tufts University. I graduated with a degree in computer science in 2020, and am currently continuing my graduate study on software systems development at the same university.

Professional Biography

Eric is a software engineering intern at Cadence Design Systems. He has been working at Cadence since June 2020. His work focuses on UI and network components of a printed circuit board (PCB) designer. Before his software engineering career, Eric used to pick grapes and teach English and mathematics.
